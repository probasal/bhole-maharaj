TRENDING POOJAS

Griha Pravesh

Pre entering Pooja for a new House or Vaastu .

Griha or Vaastu Pravesh is the occasion of entering a new house. On this day a Griha Pravesh Pooja is performed by pandit to ensure happiness and prosperity to the family and their house. In Hindu culture a Griha Pravesh Pooja is considered to be an essential step before entering a new Vaastu or house.

Vaastu Shanti

Pooja is done to get rid of negativity in the Vaastu.

Vaastu Shanti Pooja is done by every Hindu family while entering a new home. This Pooja is done for God of direction known as Vaastu Purush. In Hindu culture Vaastu Shanti Pooja is advised to be performed for new or existing Vaastu.

Satya Narayan

Satya Narayan Pooja is done on each occasion by Hindu devotees.

Satya Narayan Pooja is one of the most popular and common Pooja performed in Hindu culture. This versatile Pooja is being performed for various occasions and events. The word Satya means truth and Narayan means supreme. This Pooja is done to worship Lord Shri Satyanarayana who was the form of Lord Vishnu.

Udak Shanti

This Pooja is mainly done of Kalash with coconut on top of it.

Udak shanti Pooja has a lot of significance according to Vedic traditions. Udak shanti mantra is a long mantra with 1441 lines and is chanted on Udak Shanti Pooja day. Udak shanti mantras are proven to bring positivity, joy and success in one’s life.

Ganesh Pooja

- Lord Ganesh is worshipped in this Pooja.

Lord Ganesh is every devotee’s favourite god. Ganesh Pooja is done almost on all occasions. This Pooja is done to remove all the obstacles and bring happiness in one’s life.
